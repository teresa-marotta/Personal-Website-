About Me

Hi! I'm Teresa and I'm a second year biomedical engineering student from the University of Waterloo. In my previous co-op employments, I've worked as a Biomedical Engineering Associate and a Special Project Manager. I am interested in pursing a career in a variety of industries including healthcare, human factors, technology, and biotechnology. Overall though, I am open to working in any sector. Outside of school and work, I'm an avid reader (of any and all genres!) and I love to try new things. I enjoy spending my free time running, bouldering, and spending time with my friends and family.
