Mechanical Design Projects

Below you'll find some of the mechanical design projects I have completed at school, on work terms, and on my own.

Kidney Ultrasound Biopsy Phantom

Medical image phantoms are used to help train medical residents and studnets. This was a project I completed during my work term at Niagara Health. I worked with the Biomedical Engineering department and the Interventional Radiology department to develop the phantom using the software programs 3D Slicer, Meshmixer, and Blender in addition to SLA 3D printing.

Bluetooth Pressure Sensor

This was a project I completed while I was a member of the design team Biomecatronics. I was a part of the Pressure Sensor sub team where we worked to develop modified pressure sensor to place on patient beds in order to help limit the amount of bed sores a patient would acquire. This is the PCB that I designed for a bluetooth pressure sensor.

Sexual Health Education Kit

This was a project I completed while I was a member of the design team Medical Makers. I was a part of the Sexual Health Education project where we worked to develop 3D models of various birth control methods in addition to other models. I designed a model for a hormonal IUD (pictured above) and we used FDM 3D printing to produce the model.
